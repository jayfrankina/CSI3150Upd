Welcome to Time for Tee

A Platform For Everyday Golf Use - Featuring A Tee Time Finder, Local Golf Events, Your Own Personal Stats, and More.

Time for Tee was made for the dedicated golfers who are looking to improve their game by keeping their golf game all in one application. With Time for Tee, you are able to compare your yardages and scores to the average golfer, find tee times, and link up with others for local tournaments or leagues. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team.
